## 🪝 Prompt 1 — Scroll Stopping Hooks
**🤖 Tool:** Claude AI
**🎯 Goal:** Generate multiple hooks for the same product
to stop users from scrolling and grab attention instantly

**📝 Prompt:**
You are a UGC ad copywriter specializing in D2C skincare brands.

Write 5 different scroll-stopping hooks for "GlowPure Face Serum"
a D2C skincare product that reduces dark spots in 14 days.

Each hook must:
- Be under 15 words
- Start with a pattern interrupt (question, bold statement, or shock)
- Feel authentic and relatable — not like a traditional ad
- Target women aged 18–30 with dark spot concerns

Tone: Authentic, conversational, and attention-grabbing
Platform: Instagram Reels & Facebook Ads

**✅ Why This Prompt Works:**
- 🎯 Specifies exact product and key benefit
- 📏 Word limit keeps hooks punchy and impactful
- 💡 Pattern interrupt instruction improves hook quality
- 👩 Clear audience targeting improves relevance

## 📝 Prompt 2 — UGC Ad Scripts
**🤖 Tool:** Claude AI
**🎯 Goal:** Generate full UGC-style ad scripts in
problem-solution-CTA format for multiple platforms

**📝 Prompt:**
You are a UGC content creator and ad scriptwriter.

Write 3 different UGC-style ad scripts for "GlowPure Face Serum"
a D2C skincare product that reduces dark spots in 14 days.

Each script must follow this format:
1. Hook (attention-grabbing opening line)
2. Problem (relatable skin problem the viewer faces)
3. Solution (how GlowPure Face Serum solves it)
4. Proof (result or social proof — e.g., 10,000+ customers)
5. CTA (clear call to action)

Script lengths:
- Script 1: Instagram Reels (30 seconds, 80–100 words)
- Script 2: Facebook Ad (45 seconds, 120–150 words)
- Script 3: YouTube Shorts (60 seconds, 150–180 words)

Tone: Authentic, conversational, relatable — like a real
person sharing their experience, not a brand selling
Target audience: Women aged 18–30 with dark spot concerns

**✅ Why This Prompt Works:**
- 📋 Clear problem-solution-CTA structure ensures
  conversion-focused output
- 📏 Different word counts per platform ensures
  platform-appropriate content
- 🎭 Authentic tone instruction prevents generic ad copy
- ⭐ Proof element builds credibility and trust

## 🎨 Prompt 3 — Platform Specific Captions
**🤖 Tool:** Claude AI
**🎯 Goal:** Generate platform-specific captions with
hashtags and CTAs for each social media platform

**📝 Prompt:**
You are a social media copywriter specializing in D2C brands.

Write ad captions for "GlowPure Face Serum" for these 3 platforms:

1. Instagram Caption:
   - Max 150 words
   - Include 3 line breaks for readability
   - Add 10 relevant hashtags
   - End with strong CTA

2. Facebook Ad Caption:
   - Max 100 words
   - Conversational and benefit-focused
   - Include social proof
   - End with link CTA

3. YouTube Shorts Description:
   - Max 80 words
   - Include product link placeholder
   - Add 5 relevant tags
   - End with subscribe CTA

Product details:
- Name: GlowPure Face Serum
- Key benefit: Reduces dark spots in 14 days
- Price: ₹799
- USP: 100% natural, dermatologist tested

Tone: Authentic, engaging, and conversion-focused

**✅ Why This Prompt Works:**
- 📱 Platform-specific instructions ensure correct
  format for each platform
- 📏 Word limits prevent overly long captions
- #️⃣ Hashtag instruction improves discoverability
- 🛒 CTA instruction ensures every caption converts

## 💰 Prompt 4 — Offer & FOMO CTAs
**🤖 Tool:** Claude AI
**🎯 Goal:** Generate urgency-driven CTAs and special
offers that create FOMO and drive immediate purchases

**📝 Prompt:**
You are a conversion copywriter specializing in D2C brands.

Write 5 different urgency-driven CTA variations for
"GlowPure Skincare" brand for use in social media ads.

Each CTA must:
- Create urgency or FOMO (fear of missing out)
- Be under 20 words
- Include a special offer or limited time deal
- Feel authentic not pushy

Also write:
- 1 special offer announcement (flash sale)
- 1 first-time buyer offer
- 1 bundle deal offer

Product: GlowPure Face Serum — ₹799
Brand: GlowPure Skincare
Tone: Energetic, urgent, and authentic

**✅ Why This Prompt Works:**
- ⚡ Urgency instruction creates FOMO-driven copy
- 📏 Word limit keeps CTAs punchy and impactful
- 🎁 Multiple offer types cover different buyer stages
- 🎯 Authentic tone prevents overly salesy output
